How to Use

  1. Click "Start AR Experience" to activate your camera
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Hold your hand out with palm facing up towards the camera
  4. The 3D model will automatically appear on your palm
  5. Move your hand to see the model from different angles
  6. Use the model selector to change the 3D object
